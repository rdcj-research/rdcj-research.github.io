Formal Systems & Emergence

How does emergent structure arise within formal systems, and what roles do resonance, constraint, and geometry play in this process? This line of inquiry investigates emergence as a structural phenomenon rather than an anomaly. It focuses on how complexity arises from constrained systems, and how mathematical, geometric, and combinatorial structures give rise to unexpected order.
