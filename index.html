Open questions,
structured inquiry

Research at RDCJ Research is organised around questions rather than predefined outcomes. Each question defines a problem space rather than a thesis, providing a structured frame within which papers, artefacts, and experiments are developed. These materials are treated as provisional and revisable, and may evolve, diverge, or recombine over time as the inquiry progresses.
