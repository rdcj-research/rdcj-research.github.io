Epistemology & Scientific Method

What structural assumptions govern different epistemological frameworks, and how do these assumptions shape what can be known? This theme addresses how knowledge systems are constructed, legitimised, and maintained. Scientific methods are examined not as neutral procedures, but as symbolic and structural systems shaped by historical, cultural, and epistemic commitments.
